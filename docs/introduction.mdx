# Welcome to Volvox.Bot

Volvox.Bot is a fully hosted Discord bot platform. No installation, no configuration files, no servers to manage. Add it to your Discord server and everything just works.

<Callout type="info">
We handle hosting, updates, and infrastructure. You focus on your community.
</Callout>

## What Volvox.Bot does

| Feature | What it gives you |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Neural Chat** | AI-powered conversations in your server |
| **Active Sentry** | Auto-moderation, warnings, bans, timeouts |
| **Live Insight** | Real-time analytics and server health metrics |
| **Tickets** | Support ticket system with transcripts |
| **Community** | Leaderboards, showcases, public profiles |
| **Audit Log** | Full audit trail of all bot actions |
| **Temp Roles** | Time-limited role assignments |

## How it works

1. **Add the bot** to your server with one click
2. **Open the dashboard** at [volvox.bot](https://volvox.bot) and log in with Discord
3. **Configure** your server's settings through the web UI
4. **Done** — updates and maintenance happen automatically

Everything runs on our managed infrastructure. Your bot stays online even when your computer is off.
